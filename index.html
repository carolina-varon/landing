Bringing 'sabor' to Paris:

LoCa Arepas, is born with L & C , two best friends with the idea of bringing to France a piece of their cultures to make it feel like home. The restaurant is summarized in few words as heaven, with the main character being the famous Arepas.

What are arepas? A Colombian - Venezuelan typical dish, made a corn-based dough, melted cheese and mouthwathering filling. It is a staple in South American gastronomical culture, eaten by many everyday without distiction. Our arepas are handmade with love, and filled with the best quality ingredients.

That is why we established LoCa arepas in Paris, not only as a dream, but to establish a place in Paris to share a piece of the Latin American culture through Venezuela and Colombia; from ambiance, accent and people, to the food we serve.
